### What is BACON?

**BACON** (or B.A.C.O.N.) is a tongue-in-cheek restatement of the [[Doctrines of Grace]] (a.k.a. [[TULIP]]) using plain language.

- [[Bad People]]
- [[Already Chosen]]
- [[Completely Forgiven]]
- [[Overwhelmingly Called]]
- [[Never Falling Away]]
